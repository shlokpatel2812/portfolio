Design Principles

1. Generous Whitespace

Use negative space as a design element. Allow content to breathe with ample padding and margins.

2. Razor-Thin Borders

1px borders and lines serve as subtle dividers and structural elements without visual weight.

3. Bold Typographic Hierarchy

Contrast massive display type (60-140px) with tiny labels (11-13px) for dramatic effect.

4. Asymmetric Layouts

Break the grid intentionally. Create visual tension through unbalanced compositions.

5. Grain Texture Overlay

Subtle noise texture (3% opacity) adds depth and prevents flat, sterile appearance.

Animation Guidelines

Duration

Fast transitions: 0.3s | Standard: 0.4s | Slow: 0.6-1.2s

Easing

power3.out for entrances | power2.in/out for overlays | elastic.out for magnetic effects

Page Transitions

Overlay wipes down (scaleY: 1, 0.4s), content changes, overlay retracts (scaleY: 0, 0.4s)
